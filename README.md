# ☕ Global Coffee Shop Sales Performance Analysis (2023)
### Revenue, Customer Behavior & Weather Impact Analytics

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a global coffee shop retail dataset covering 20,000 transactions across 45 stores in 9 cities and 4 countries during 2023.

The objective was to simulate how retail analytics teams, consulting firms, and business intelligence departments evaluate:

- Revenue performance
- Customer purchasing behavior
- Loyalty program effectiveness
- Weather-driven demand shifts
- Store operational efficiency
- Product profitability and menu engineering

The project was completed using Excel for business analysis, MySQL for advanced SQL analytics, and Tableau for executive dashboarding & storytelling.

## 📊 Dataset Overview

This dataset simulates real-world Point-of-Sale (POS) coffee shop transactions.

The data captures how:

- Weather conditions influence purchasing behavior
- Store location affects pricing strategy
- Loyalty discounts impact revenue
- Customer demographics affect sales performance
- Seasonal trends drive demand fluctuations

#### Time Period
- January 2023 – December 2023

## 📊 Tableau Dashboard Structure

### 🔹 Dashboard 1 — Executive Revenue Overview
**Business Question:** Where is revenue coming from?

**KPIs:**
- Total Revenue
- Total Transactions
- Average Transaction Value
- Average Basket Size
- Unique Customers

**Charts:**
- Revenue by Country
- Revenue by City
- Revenue Contribution by Product Category
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Store Type

> 💡 **Key Insights:**
> • USA generates the highest revenue
> • Coffee products dominate total sales
> • Revenue remains stable throughout the year
> • Standalone stores contribute most revenue

---

### 🔹 Dashboard 2 — Customer & Loyalty Analytics
**Business Question:** Who are our most valuable customers?

**Charts:**
- Revenue by Age Group
- Average Transaction Value by Age Group
- Loyalty vs Non-Loyalty Comparison
- Discount Adoption Heatmap
- Customer Segmentation Treemap

> 💡 **Key Insights:**
> • 25–34 age group contributes the highest revenue
> • Older customers spend more per visit
> • Loyalty customers drive repeat business
> • Discounts influence purchasing behavior differently across age groups

---

### 🔹 Dashboard 3 — Weather & Seasonal Intelligence
**Business Question:** How does weather impact demand?

**Charts:**
- Revenue by Weather Condition
- Weather vs Product Category Heatmap
- Temperature vs Cold Beverage Sales
- City Weather Sensitivity Analysis
- Holiday Revenue Analysis

> 💡 **Key Insights:**
> • Rainy weather drives strong sales performance
> • Beverage preferences shift significantly with weather
> • Temperature influences cold beverage demand
> • Weather sensitivity varies across cities

---

### 🔹 Dashboard 4 — Store Performance & Operational Efficiency
**Business Question:** Which stores and markets perform best?

**Charts:**
- Revenue Per Store by Country
- Store Type Comparison

> 💡 **Key Insights:**

> • Peak hours drive operational efficiency decisions

---

### 🔹 Dashboard 5 — Product & Menu Engineering Optimization
**Business Question:** Which products should be promoted, repriced, or removed?

- Demand vs Revenue Matrix
- Top Revenue Products

- Product Performance Scorecard

**Strategic Segments:**
- Promote — High Demand + High Revenue
- Reprice — High Demand + Low Revenue
- Maintain — Moderate Demand + Strong Revenue
- Review — Low Demand + Low Revenue

> 💡 **Key Insights:**
> • Several premium coffee products dominate revenue
> • Some bakery products drive traffic but generate limited revenue
> • Menu engineering opportunities exist for pricing optimization
